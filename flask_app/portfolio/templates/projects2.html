Parkinson app

This is a classification problem where I had to predict if the person has Parkinson or not. The app designed has many features as under:

  • Scatter plots between dependent features for EDA
  • Looking at the Intercorealtion matrix
  • Best models
  • Best Paratmenters with Gridsearch
  • Result of the test prediction
